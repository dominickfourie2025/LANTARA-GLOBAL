WHY LANTARA?

We understand the unique demands of international education recruitment. Lantara Global delivers reliable, ethical, and expertly managed placement services — for schools that demand quality and teachers who deserve exceptional support.

Rigorous Screening

Every candidate undergoes a thorough multi-stage evaluation — from qualifications and experience to background checks and trial lessons — ensuring only the most capable teachers reach your school.

Global Recruitment

We source qualified native English-speaking teachers across the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, South Africa, and Ireland — providing your school with a diverse, high-calibre candidate pool.

Fully Supported

Our commitment doesn't end at placement. We provide continuous support throughout the contract — helping schools and teachers navigate visa applications, cultural transitions, and any challenges that arise.

About Us

ABOUT LANTARA GLOBAL

Lantara Global was founded on a commitment to ethical, transparent, and professional international education recruitment. We bridge the gap between ambitious Chinese schools and highly qualified native English-speaking teachers from across the globe.

With deep experience navigating both Western and Chinese educational environments, we understand what it takes to make a placement successful — for the school, the teacher, and most importantly, the students.

Our dedicated team provides end-to-end support, ensuring every placement is managed with the diligence, care, and cultural sensitivity that international recruitment demands.
