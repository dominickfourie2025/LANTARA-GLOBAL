OUR PROCESS

A Professional Recruitment Journey from Initial Enquiry to Successful Placement

Every recruitment project follows a structured, transparent process designed to save schools time while ensuring candidates receive a professional experience from beginning to end.

01

Consultation

We begin by understanding your school's recruitment objectives, teaching requirements, preferred qualifications and hiring timeline.

02

Candidate Search

Suitable teachers are sourced through our international recruitment network and carefully screened before presentation.

03

Interviews

Schools interview shortlisted candidates, ensuring every applicant aligns with both academic expectations and organisational culture.

04

Placement & Support

Once an offer is accepted, we continue supporting both the school and teacher throughout the onboarding process.
